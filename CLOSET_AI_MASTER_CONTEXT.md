# Product Vision

ClosetAI is an AI-powered personal fashion assistant.

The mission:

Create a smart wardrobe that understands the user, their clothes, their style, and their lifestyle.

ClosetAI is not only a digital closet.

It is:

"My personal AI stylist."

The user should feel:

"I have a fashion expert that knows my wardrobe and helps me decide what to wear."

# Photo Upload System

Important feature:

Users can upload photos of their clothes.

Sources:

- Camera
- Gallery


Future AI:

When a user uploads an outfit photo:

The system separates:

Example:

Photo:

White shirt
Blue jeans
White sneakers


Creates:

Shirt item

Pants item

Shoes item


Each item should keep its real image.

---

# Visual Wardrobe Rule

Important:

If a user uploaded a real clothing image:

Show the real image inside the closet.

Do not replace it with:

- Text only
- Empty placeholders


Example:

Correct:

Shirts:

[Real shirt image]


Incorrect:

"White Shirt"

without image.
